What should we know about Mount Merapi?

The website content covers the key aspects that give identity to Mount Merapi and shaped its history.

New York State

Merapi’s eruption in 2010 with rapid onset and large magnitude had displaced almost 400,000 people and posed great challenges in evacuation processes (Mei, et al., 2013, p. 2). Despite these impacts, most villagers returned to their settlements and developed various activities in areas situated only 5 kms from the volcano’s crater (Sagala, et al., 2012, p. 1; Davis, 2013).

Community

It is believed by anthropologists, that Merapi community is the descendents of ancient Mataram Hindu society who moved from Central Java to East Java due to eruption in 1006 A.D.(Triyoga, 1991, p. 21).Influenced by Hindu-Buddhas, Merapi community perceived the volcano as a sacred kingdom of gods and ancestral spirits (Triyoga, 1991, p. 35).

Gate Keeper

As a royal servant appointed directly by Sultan , Mbah Maridjan was obliged to prepare and lead the court’s spiritual rite Labuhan Gunung on the slopes of Merapi. The aim is to maintain the harmonious relationship between the deities living in Mount Merapi, those in the South Sea, and the Sultanate of Yogyakarta.(Maarif, et al., 2012, p. 7).
